Chicken and Broccoli

Description

A simple yet healthy meal. None of the frills (or taste), but it will satisfy you all the same.

Ingredients

  • Chicken Breast
  • Rice
  • Broccoli
  • Salt and pepper (if you're into that sort of thing)

Steps

  1. Bake chicken in oven at 375 for 35 minutes
  2. Steam broccoli
  3. Cook rice
  4. Put it all on a plate and eat it!
