Pizza!

Description

A simple, classic pizza. You can't go wrong here. If you put that much chese on anything it is bound to taste good!

Ingredients

  • Pizza Dough
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Mozzarella Cheese

Steps

  1. Make pizza dough thin
  2. Shape the dough how you would like
  3. Pour sauce over dough, leaving some space around the edges
  4. Cover with the mozz
  5. Cook in oven at high heat until it looks done
