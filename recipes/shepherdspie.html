Shepherd's Pie!

Description

The Best Classic Shepherd's Pie - AKA Shepards Pie or Cottage Pie. Ground Beef (or lamb) with vegetables in a rich gravy, topped with cheesy mashed potatoes, and baked.

Ingredients

  • Olive Oil
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Carrots
  • Rosemary
  • Thyme
  • Parsley
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Meat (Beef or Lamb work best!)
  • Potatoes
  • Worcestershire Sauce
  • Tomato paste

Steps

  1. Heat oil and garlic in a pan
  2. Add carrots to pan
  3. Add meat to pan
  4. Add tomato paste and worcestershire
  5. Boil potatoes and mash
  6. Add meat to pan and put potatoes on top
  7. Bake and enjoy!
